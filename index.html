My Story

Joana's Custom Candles was started by me... Joana! The beauty of the art of candles motivates me with every candle.

Each of my candles is handcrafted using premium soy wax, natural fragrances, and sustainable materials to create a beautiful design!

My passion for candle making began in my humble abode, making candles for my family! I decided to turn my passion into a venture! I strive for each one of my candles to bring love, warmth, and comfort into every home!

From relaxing lavender to invigorating citrus, my candles offer something special for every occasion. I love to take custom orders - especially challenging ones - to provide an exquisite product!
